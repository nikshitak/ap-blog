Welcome!

To all the high school students out there who aren't sure which AP's to take, hello! This website is really to help you see which AP classes are worth your time and money in high school. Even if you want to stand out to colleges on your applications, they don't want to see you take 20 APs and fail all of them.

Things to Keep in Mind

  • These descriptions either came from the College Board website or they're my summary of the class since I've taken it.
  • Because some of the descriptions are my own, these are biased and opinionated, and you should take classes that you like even if it's difficult.
  • All of these classes will seem challenging to someone but easy to another; at the end of the day, the difficulty of these classes depends on how you study for them and your teacher.
  • Click on the Course Exam and Description image on each subject to go to the Overview of each AP Class by Collegeboard. There, you'll be able to look at the Course and Exam Description as well as other documents that explain the class more in-depth.
